About us

With a family tradition of over 70 years in the real estate industry in Brazil, Tecbos Empreendimentos Imobiliários was born in 1992 with the objective to continue the family legacy in the city of São Paulo.

Over our 30+ years, we have participated in the development of projects throughout the State of São Paulo, accumulating a total of ten businesses in our portfolio. Today, Tecbos operates in the development and management of properties in Brazil, and in Montreal, Canada.
